On the special-promo and holiday batches, yes — the rate you pay at checkout is the rate that recurs. The standard monthly KVM page is also recurring at the listed price, but the per-year cost is higher than the specials. The headline specials are where the real VPS discount lives.

**Can I upgrade my plan later without losing the discount?**

You can upgrade at any time through the SolusVM panel — it's a reboot's worth of downtime, roughly a minute. The catch is the upgrade is billed at the current plan's price, not the promo rate you originally locked in. So if you grabbed a $11.29/year NYE box and want to bump it to the 2 GB tier, you'd pay the difference at the standard-tier rate, not the promo. My advice: buy the tier you'll actually need for the next 12 months, not the tier you need today.
